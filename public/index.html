Web3 Comprehensive Guide

What is Web3?

Web3 is a decentralized network based on distributed ledger technology, that is, the next-generation network form of Web2 Internet we currently use. Web3 utilizes blockchain technology to build decentralized applications (DApps), smart contracts, and NFTs, bringing innovative directions to many industries.

Why is Web3 Important?

Web3 has freed itself from the read-and-write restrictions of Web1 and Web2, restoring people's rights and enhancing users' privacy and security on the internet. Meanwhile, Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) has been incubated as a more fair and transparent social organization, bringing new possibilities for social operation.

What is a Web3 Wallet? What is Gate Web3 Wallet?

The Web3 wallet is an important tool leading to Web3, providing users with innovative and diverse crypto services such as managing crypto assets, interacting with decentralized applications, storing non-fungible tokens (NFTs), creating on-chain identities, and collaborating with communities.

Gate.io independently developed the Gate Web3 Wallet which is a safe and fast non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet. It serves the requirements of Web3 users to trade cryptocurrency anytime and anywhere, and truly brings Web3 into the daily life of the public.
